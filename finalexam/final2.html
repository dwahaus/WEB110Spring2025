Scott Pilgrim Trivia!

Top 5 Facts

  1. The film originally had Scott end up with Knives, but this was changed after test screenings.
  2. Each fight represents a different video game genre.
  3. Michael Cera trained for months to do fight choreography.
  4. The music was written by real bands. (e.g. Beck, and Metric.)
  5. The sound effects were pulled directly from retro video games. (e.g. Zelda.)
